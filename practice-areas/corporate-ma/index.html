🏢 Case Study: American founder setting up SRL in Milan
David wants to incorporate an Italian SRL for his B2B software business. He's a US citizen with codice fiscale, Italian permesso di soggiorno, and €15,000 to invest.

Notaio (atto costitutivo): €2,000 + IVA = €2,440 | Registro Imprese: €200 | Tassa concessione: €310 | CCIAA diritto annuale: €160 | Commercialista year 1: €1,200 | Partita IVA: Free
Total setup: ~€4,310 (plus €15,000 capital, of which €3,750 = 25% must be deposited at deed, rest within 90 days).

As sole administrator (amministratore unico), David pays himself a salary subject to IRPEF + INPS Gestione Separata (26.23%). Company profits subject to IRES 24% then 26% on dividends. Consider impatriati regime (art. 5 D.Lgs. 209/2023) for 50% income exemption in first 5 years.

Sources: CC artt. 2325–2328 (SPA), 2462–2471 (SRL); L. 27/2012 art. 3 (SRLS); D.Lgs. 344/2003 (IRES); D.Lgs. 446/1997 (IRAP); L. 190/2014 (Patent Box); D.Lgs. 209/2023 (impatriati); DL 201/2011 (ACE — abolished 2024).
