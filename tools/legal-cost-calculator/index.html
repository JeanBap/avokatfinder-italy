Property Purchase Cost Calculator

Key rules (DPR 131/1986 + D.Lgs. 23/2011):
• Prima casa concession: imposta di registro 2% (min €1,000), must be principal residence within 18 months of purchase.
• Seconda casa: imposta di registro 9% (min €1,000) on cadastral value.
• New builds from developer: IVA 4% (prima casa) or 10% (seconda casa) on purchase price — registro drops to €200 fixed.
• Imposta ipotecaria + catastale: €50+€50 (prima casa) or €50+€50 (seconda casa from private); €200+€200 from developer.
• Notaio emoluments follow DM 265/2012 sliding scale + IVA 22%.

Company Formation Cost Estimator

Formation route: All companies registered via Registro delle Imprese (Camere di Commercio). Online formation available for SRL via pratica telematica. Deed (atto costitutivo) requires notaio; SRLS uses standard deed free of charge (no notaio emolument). Codice fiscale (fiscal code) required for all founders.

Avvocato Fee Estimator (DM 55/2014 parametri)

DM 55/2014 (parametri forensi): Italy abolished mandatory fee tariffs in 2012 (DL 1/2012). Fees are now freely negotiated; DM 55/2014 provides reference parameters for court-awarded costs. Hourly rates typically €150–€500/hr for senior avvocati in major cities. IVA 22% applies to legal fees. Cassa Forense contribution (4% welfare surcharge) may be added.
